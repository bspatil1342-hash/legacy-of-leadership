About

Late Shri Ajit Dada Pawar

Known for his dedication to public service, leadership, and commitment towards the development of Maharashtra. His vision, administrative abilities, and contribution to society continue to inspire future generations.

A personality that has understood the collective consciousness of Maharashtra! Shri Ajit Pawar is a shining presence on the political horizon of Maharashtra, someone who dedicates 16-17 hours every day to serve the people of Maharashtra and help them solve their problems. His astounding and clear vision of development has influenced Maharashtra’s political and social life over the last three decades.

Journey of Leadership

Early Years

Ajit Anantrao Pawar was born on 22 July 1959 in Deolali Pravara, Maharashtra,to Anantrao and Ashatai Pawar. In 1985,he married Sunetra Pawar (née Patil), the sister of Padamsinh Bajirao Patil, a senior NCP leader. The couple had two sons, Parth and Jay Pawar.

Public Service

Following the footsteps of his uncle Sharad Pawar in the Indian National Congress, Ajit Pawar made his first foray into politics in 1982 when he was elected to the board of a cooperative sugar factory.

A Political Hand

On 23 November 2019, Ajit Pawar joined the BJP-led government and became Deputy Chief Minister, claiming majority support from NCP MLAs. However, the government collapsed within 80 hours, leading to his resignation. He then returned to the NCP and was reappointed as Deputy Chief Minister in the Maha Vikas Aghadi government in December 2019.

A Lasting Legacy

The lasting legacy of Hon'ble Deputy Chief Minister Ajit Dada Pawar is reflected in his dedication to public service, development, and the welfare of the people of Maharashtra.
