About

Late Shri Ajit Dada Pawar

Late Shri Ajit Dada Pawar was known for his dedication to public service, leadership, and commitment towards the development of Maharashtra. His vision, administrative abilities, and contribution to society continue to inspire future generations.

This website is created as a respectful tribute on the occasion of his First Death Anniversary, honouring his life, leadership, and lasting legacy.
